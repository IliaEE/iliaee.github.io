Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 06, 2026

Drawee ("we," "our," "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

1. Information We Collect

Drawee collects minimal information necessary to provide our AR drawing services. We may collect:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version for compatibility
  • Usage Analytics: How you use the app to improve user experience
  • Camera Access: Required for AR drawing features (images are not stored or transmitted)
  • Photo Library Access: To allow you to select images for tracing (with your permission)
  • Account Information: Email or user ID if you create an account
  • Drawing Preferences: Your saved settings and preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our AR drawing services
  • Personalize your drawing experience
  • Analyze app usage and performance
  • Process subscription payments through Apple
  • Send updates or offers (with your consent)
  • Provide customer support

3. Data Storage and Security

Your drawings and data are stored locally on your device. We do not upload or store your drawings on our servers unless you explicitly choose to back them up. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, changes, or loss.

4. Third-Party Services

Drawee uses the following third-party services to provide and improve our app:

  • RevenueCat: For subscription management and payment processing
  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements to free users
  • PostHog: For analytics and app performance monitoring

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing their use of your information. We recommend reviewing their policies:

  • RevenueCat Privacy Policy: revenuecat.com/privacy
  • Google AdMob Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy
  • PostHog Privacy Policy: posthog.com/privacy

5. Sharing Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only with:

  • Service providers who help us operate the app (hosting, analytics, payment processing)
  • Legal authorities if required by law or to protect our rights

6. Cookies and Tracking

Drawee may use cookies or similar tracking technologies to improve your experience and gather analytics about app usage.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of your personal data
  • Ask us to delete your data
  • Opt out of marketing emails
  • Disable analytics tracking in app settings

To exercise these rights, contact us at wotaapphelp@gmail.com

8. Age Requirement

Drawee is not directed to children under 13 years of age. You must be at least 13 years old to use the app. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Camera and Photos Access

Drawee requires camera access to provide AR drawing functionality. Your camera feed is processed in real-time on your device and is not recorded, stored, or transmitted. Photos from your library are accessed only when you choose to select an image for tracing. We do not store, transmit, or share your photos.

10. International Users

If you are accessing Drawee from outside Estonia, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in Estonia or other countries where our service providers operate.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app or on this page, and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:

Email: wotaapphelp@gmail.com
